About AI System

Brain Tumor AI is a Deep Learning based web application developed to detect brain tumors from MRI scans automatically.

The system uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and TensorFlow to classify MRI images into different categories with high accuracy.

Supported Classes:

  • Glioma Tumor
  • Meningioma Tumor
  • Pituitary Tumor
  • No Tumor

The project also generates Grad-CAM Heatmaps and PDF Medical Reports for better visualization and understanding.
